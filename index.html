About

From my teenage years, I've always been drawn to the world of computers. I still remember configuring the control panel on my old Windows 7 PC. There was just something so satisfying about adjusting the settings and really getting to understand how the system worked. That sense of discovery and curiosity has stuck with me ever since my teenage years.

Later, as I got a laptop from my family that I named Eva, a friend introduced me to Linux, starting with Kali Linux for delving into ethical hacking. I began experimenting with various Linux distributions. From Ubuntu to Fedora, I expanded my knowledge with each installation.
